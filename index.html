HMONG 18 DESKTOP & MOBILE HOMEPAGE REDESIGN

A redesign to bring forward important content, optimize navigation for to help users find key services, and visual illustrate how donations are making a positive impact on the community.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

ROLE / TEAM
  • Team size: 3
  • My Role: Design Lead
  • Co-Lead Project Management
  • Created Storytelling + Pitch Deck
TIMELINE
  • Three week timeline:
  • Week one: Research, Analysis, Align on actionable insights
  • Week two: Wireframing and Prototyping
  • Week three: User testing + Interating to High-Fidelity Prototype
TOOLS
  • Figma
  • FigJam
  • Trello
  • Google Suite

THE OPPORTUNITY

The Hmong 18 Council website is the life-line to the Hmong community providing guidance in all aspects of their lives from health education to marital and family support.

  • Their website has not been updated in over 5 years
  • doesnt address basic accessibility issues
  • lacks information on services provided and
  • low awareness on how donations dollars support the community they serve

OUR SOLUTION

A redesign of the Hmong 18 Council website and mobile experience are essential in ensuring the organization can effectively serve the Hmong community.

CLEAR MISSION & GOALS OF ORGANIZATION

Bring forth the organizations mission to improve trust and relationships with community.

ENHANCE NAVIGATION & FINDABILITY OF KEY SERVICES

Refine existing navigation to highlight most common and important categories, including services offered.

VISUAL EVIDENCE OF COMMUNITY IMPACT

Replace images to better showcase the work the organization does in the community.

WEEK ONE

RESEARCH & ANALYSIS

SURVEY

survey with adults between 18-45 Hmong community members or closely connected Understand current views towards cultural non-profits, awareness and engagement with the Hmong 18 Council Initial reactions to their website.

STAKEHOLDER INTERVIEW

with the current President/CEO and Education Lead from Hmong Council 18 to understand organization's mission, goals, purpose for website, unmet goals from website.

HEURISTIC EVALUATION

to translate content to Hmong to address needs of their target users Include a search option to help users find specific types of information faster and Apply consistent branding including using more universal icons to help increase recognition among all users.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

purpose and mission Bilingual option offered on the site Quality visual evidence of how the organization gives back to the community.

USER TESTING

ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS

LESSONS LEARNED + FUTURE OPPORTUNITIES

The biggest lesson learned with this project was sticking with our initial choice to complete this redesign. We had trouble getting in touch with the right people to talk to about the project, which set us back in our timeline. We were eventually were able to complete a stakeholder interview which gave us some really good insights in terms of why this website is vital to the community.
